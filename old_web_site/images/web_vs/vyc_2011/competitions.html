Essay competition

Everyone possesses a huge potential to excel. Now its time to gear up and channelize this potential in a constructive way. The Essay Writing Competition is mainly focused on enabling you to explore the "real" secrets of Life. Just pen down your ideas coupled with that of Swami Vivekananda's.

Topics:
  1. Swami Vivekananda: An Inspiration for the Youth.
  2. What is real happiness?
Word Limit: 1500 words

Submission mail-id: vivekananda.samiti.iitk@gmail.com

Deadline: 11:59 pm, 15th January, 2011.

P.S. Prizes will be given for both the topics.
