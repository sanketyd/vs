If you are interested to learn sanskrit, most ancient and scientific language on this earth, here is an opportunity for you..




About the organisation:

Sanskrita Bharati, an NGO, dedicated for reviving this language plans to conduct a 10 day spoken sanskrit class in our campus. Sanskrita Bharati's classes are entirely fun-filled and the method of teaching is entirely different from the traditional rote learning methodology. By following a unique and effective method of teaching Sanskrit through conversation, Sanskrita Bharati (SB) has reached out to over 8 million people.

Unique Features:
1) No prior knowledge in sanskrit is required.
2) No need of pen and paper (inside the class).
3) Sanskrit is taught through games, stories, charts, practical examples and other teaching aids.
4) You will be able to communicate in this language by the end of 10 days.
5) There is no fee for the classes.
6) The initial 10-day classes are followed by regular week class for further study of sanskrit language.
