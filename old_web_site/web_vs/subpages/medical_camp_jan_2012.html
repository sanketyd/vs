Brief reports on Medical health camp and Eye camp


As a part of celebration of the 150th Birth Anniversary of Swami Vivekananda, we are organizing the following community service activities in association with Ramakrishna Mission Hospital, Kanpur and Shiksha Sopan, Kanpur.

: Vivekananda Samiti, IIT Kanpur organized a Medical health camp for about 375 under-privileged children and their parents in and around the campus. This program was conducted on Sunday, 12th Febrauary 2012 9:30 am to 1 pm with the help of Ramakrishna Mission Hospital, Kanpur at Shiksha Sopan Vidyalaya at Barasirohi village, near IIT Kanpur. This camp was arranged with the help of voluntary service of a team of four competent doctors: Dr. G.K. Tiwari, Dr. R.M. Gupta, Dr. Mohita Gulati and Dr. Sutapa Mandal, all of whom offer their services regularly at Ramakrishna Mission Hospital. This camp was inaugurated by Dr. H.C. Verma, President, Shiksha Sopan and Swami Tejomayananda, Secretary, Ramakrishna Mission Hospital. Among the other eminent guests included Dr. O.P. Mishra, Health Center, IITK who provided free medicines. Over 35 comitted volunteers from Vivekananda Samiti and Shiksha sopan rendered their voluntary services to carry out the camp smoothly.

Most of the patients which included over 250 children were found victims of malnutrition thereby causing low haemoglobin and less immunity. Lot of patients complained of cold, skin problems and digestion problems. The patients were given free consultation and medication for 4 days and were advised to continue their treatment at RKM Hospital. view pics


: An Eye Camp was organized at Navasrijan Vidyalaya, Ratanpur near Nankari on Sunday 19th Feb., 2012 from 9:30 am to 1 pm. Dr. Anju Mishra with her team offered her voluntary service. About 75 patients came for examination. About 90% patients were found suffering from long or short-sightedness. They were provided with glasses at a subsidized price. Four Patients suffering from cataract were offered surgery and medication without any charges. view pics
