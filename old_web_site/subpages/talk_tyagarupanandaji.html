About the Speaker:

Swami Tyagarupananda is an eminent scholar and an excellent orator of the Ramakrishna Order. He is the Principal of the Rmakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, the residential college at Belur Math, Howrah, West Bengal, since 2005. He earned his PhD degree in Economics from the Calcutta University in 1993. He has delivered many lectures at several renowned institutes and government organizations . In addition to classes in economics he conducts regular classes on the Indian religious texts for brahmacharins.
