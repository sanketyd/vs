College Events

Quiz
  • Eligibility: Students from any year of B.Tech or M.Tech.
  • The quiz will offer an individual to explore.
  • Themes:
    1. Indian Culture and History
    2. Life and Works of Swami Vivekananda
    3. General awareness.
  • There will be only 1 round.
  • The participants will be asked to take a written quiz objective type with multiple choice questions for 30 to 45 mins.
The Youth Leadership Challenge
  • Eligibility: Students from any year of B.Tech or M.Tech.
  • Participants will be judged on the social and leadership qualities.
  • There will be 4 rounds.
  • Round 1: Online questionnaire round for the short listing of participants.
  • Round 2: Group Discussion, The participants will be given a topic and will go through with Group Discussion.
  • Round 3: Case study, Topics will announced on website.
  • A presentation should given before the judges.
  • Times limit for presentation is 10 min.
  • Round 4: Personal Interview, Participant will be asked some questions by our panel of judges.

    • Social Innovation Challenge
  • Eligibility: Students from any year of B.Tech or M.Tech.
  • The quiz will offer an individual to explore with the fire to excel in field of business and economics.
  • Theme: The individual will have to show a sense of social welfare with their idea of incorporating that in a society
  • There will be 4 rounds
  • Round 1: Participants will be asked to submit an abstract on the given topic. The word limit can be defined later ( say 300 to 350 words)
  • Round 2: The participant will be asked to give an entrepreneurial idea.
  • Round 3:The selected will have to show their capabilities in giving feasibility to their idea and team formation.
  • Round 4: The selected candidates will give a final touch to their idea in terms of fund raising and marketing.
    • Movie Making
  • The runtime for films should not exceed 10 minutes.
  • The film must be based on ideas that are unique, novel and natural.
  • Please avoid obscenity, excessive violence, and photo slideshows. In such a case, the submission will be rejected.
  • Please refrain from making advertisements, remixes and spoofs.
  • Films in languages apart from English & Malayalam should be accompanied with subtitles.
  • Movies should be submitted to vsamti@iitk.ac.in by 01.01.2014
