School Events

Poster Making
  • Students from Class 9th and 10th would be eligible.
  • A school can send its team comprising of maximum 3 students.
  • Medium: English or Hindi.
  • One poster per team.
Quiz
  • Students from Class 9th and 10th would be eligible.
  • A school can send its team comprising of maximum 3 students.
  • Medium: English and Hindi.
  • Theme:
    1. liIndian Culture and History,
    2. Life and Works of Swami Vivekananda.
    3. General awareness.
  • There will be two rounds:
    1. Preliminary Round:
      1. Maximum time limit: 30 minutes.
      2. Total number of questions: 30.
      3. Marking Scheme: Correct Answer: +3, Incorrect Answer: -1 (?). Not attempted: 0.
      4. After combining marks of all the participants of the same team, top 5 teams will go to the next round.
    2. Final Round:
      1. Audio-Visual rounds (2-3 cyclical rounds).
        Marking Scheme: Correct: +5, Incorrect or not attempted: 0, Bonus: +2)
      2. Buzzer-round.
        3. Marking Scheme: Correct: +5, Incorrect or not attempted: 0)
      3. Rapid-Fire Round (10 Questions, time: 5 minutes for each team.)
      4. Total Duration: 15 minutes.
Caption Writing
  • Medium: English or Hindi.
  • Participants of other events can also participate in this one (?).
  • Caption writing: Students need to give an appropriate/interesting title to the provided image/drawing or graphic. It is a short contest similar to those frequently found in puzzle/game sections of magazines and newspapers.
  • Time Limit: 20 minutes.
Relay
  • A set of interconnected questions that are to be answered stepwise (answer of a question is part of the next question. and so on.)
  • Medium: English or Hindi.
Extempore
  • Students from Class 9th and 10th would be eligible.
  • A school can send its team comprising of maximum 2 students
  • Medium: English or Hindi.
  • After the topic is given participant has 45 seconds to think on the topic.
  • Then the participant will be given 2 minutes (30 seconds extra, if needed) to speak.
  • Exceeding the allotted time (2min 30 sec) may lead to marks-deduction.
Essay Writing
  • Students from Class 9th and 10th would be eligible
  • A school can send maximum 3 representatives
  • Medium: English or Hindi.
  • Word limit would be maximum 500 words
